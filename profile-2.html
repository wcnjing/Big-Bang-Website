ABOUT

Big Bang(빅뱅) is a South Korean boy band formed by YG Entertainment. They debuted on the 19th of August, 2006 and are still an active band. This group consists of G-dragon, T.O.P, Taeyang and Daesung. When the group was first formed, it consisted of 6 members. However, due to the elimination process, only 5 of them were able to debut. In 2019, Seungri, who was originally apart of the band, announced that he was leaving the entertainment industry.

They are known as 'The Kings of K-pop', being the first K-pop Idol group to have an international audience. They rose to fame with their trend-setting music experimentation, self production, and unbeatable stage presence. In the past, idol groups were seen as talentless. When BigBang garnered attention, they proved this sterotype wrong and people started to appreciate K-pop more. They first recieved attention when they released their hit song 'Lies' (거짓말) in 2007. They popularised and started a lot of the K-pop trends seen today, such as the 'lightstick', as well as spunky hair colours. Even in 2022, after being inactive for over 4 years, Big Bang still managed to break many records with their comeback single 'Still Life'(봄여름가을겨울).

Individual Profiles

개별 프로필

G-DRAGON

Stage name: G-dragon (지드래곤)
Real name: Kwon Ji-yong (권지용)
Born: 18 August 1988
Height: 177 cm (5'10")
Weight: 58 kg (128lbs)
Blood type: A

G-dragon is the leader of bigbang. He stands out with his unique and high-pitched voice. A talented singer, songwrite and rapper, his stage name is "G-DRAGON". He chose this stage name because in Korean, "Ji-yong" means dragon, and "Ji-yong" is pronouced in english as "G-yong." He started as a trainee at S.M. Entertainment at the young age of 8 years. Later, he will go on to become a trainee at YG Entertainment along side another BigBang member, Taeyang. They trained together for about 6 years to debut. The two was initially meant to debut as a hip-hop duo, "GDYB", but the plan changed. Instead, BigBang was formed when 3 more members were added. As the leader of BigBang, he contributes heavily. Known for his exceptional song-writing skills, many of BigBang's songs are written by him. Having been song-writing for decades, he is sometimes called a "song-writing genius".

The main rapper is also a fashion icon. He has appeared in numerous leading fashion magazines, such as "Vogue" and "Elle". He is also an ambassador for Chanel, a luxury fashion brand. He has always made a big impact in trend-setting, due to his distinctive choices of hairstyles and clothing.

Later on in 2009, he made his long awaited solo debut with the album "Heartbreaker". Heartbreaker was a huge hit, selling over 300,000 copies and winning Album of the Year at the 2009 Mnet Asia Music Awards. In 2013, he would release his second album, "Coup d'Etat". this record-breaking album recieved numerous awards, and he went on to reach an international audience with this new release. In the same year, he would go on his first solo concert tour, called "One of a kinda World Tour". The tour was the first headlining concert tour by a South Korean artist. His next tour was followed shortly after in 2017, "Act III: M.O.T.T.E World Tour".

Due to Korea's military laws, the rapper had to serve his mandatory military service in 2018, and completed his service in late 2019. Since then, he has been inactive in the industry. However, he is set to release a long awaited solo album soon in 2023.


TAEYANG

Stage name: Taeyang (태양)
Real name: Dong Young-bae (동영배)
Born: 18 May 1988
Height: 174 cm (5'9")
Weight: 56 kg (123lbs)
Blood type: B

Dong Young-bae is the Main vocalist and Main dancer of Big Bang. He is known for having a strong and soulful voice. Hence, his Japanese stage name is "SOL". His style in singing and dancing is highly recognised, and are favourites among other idols. His Japnese stage name is derived from "Taeyang", which means the sun in korean. He dreamt of making people "warm with my music, let it be a light." He frequently references this in his albums and songs. He started training at YG Entertainment since he was 12, along side fellow Big Bang member, G-dragon. He is looked up upon by many of the vocalists in the K-pop indsutry today.

Taeyang is the only married Big Bang member. In 2018, he married Min Hyo-rin. The two have been dating since 2013. The wedding was attended by all Big Bang members. The couple had their first son in December 2021.

In 2008, Taeyang made his solo debut with his first mini album 'Hot'. The extended play(EP) was well recieved by both fans and critics, winnning 2 trophies from the Korean Music Awards. Later in 2010, Taeyang would release his first full-length solo album, "Solar". This album would go on to win him "Best Male Artist" award in 2010 at the Mnet Asian Music Awards. It was a huge sucess, and peaked at the number one position on the Gaon Album Chart. The singer would have his first solo tour in 2014, along with the release of his second album, "Rise". The Rise World Tour visited 9 countries and 16 cites in Asia. That year, his lead single "Eyes, Nose, Lips" won him numerous awards at the Mnet Asian Music Awards, and was the second best-performing single of 2014 in South Korea.

The dancer enlisted in the military in 2018, along side fellow member Daesung and G-dragon. He was released in late 2019, and has yet to make a release ever since. Earlier in 2022, he has announced he will be releasing a new solo album in the near future, and many fans are excited for his return.

DAESUNG

Stage name: Daesung (대성)
Real name: Kang Dae-sung (강대성)
Born: 26 April 1989
Height: 178 cm (5'10")
Weight: 63 kg (139lbs)
Blood type: O

Daesung is the Leader vocalist in Bigbang. He is also known as the 'maknae'(the youngest) in Big Bang after ex-member, Seungri left. In Japan, he is more well-known as "D-Lite", his japanese stage name. Daesung has a strong, full-ranged and distinctive voice. He is well known for his unique singing style. His name, "Daesung", translates to "big voice". He is nicknamed "the smiling angel", as he has a warm and contagious smile. He is a talented vocalist and drummer. He is seen playing the drums in live performances such as 'SOBER'. He joined YG Entertainment, along side Seungri, T.O.P and Hyun-Seung in 2006. Seungri, T.O.P and Daesung trained for a year before they debuted. His Jpanese stage name "D-Lite", comes from the word "Delight" in english.

Daesung is also well-known in the industry for being a main cast member of the variety show "Family Outing". He gained popularity through his well-recieved humor while being a guest on numerous variety shows, such as "Running man", "Night after night" and "Happy Together".

In 2013, he released his first solo album in Japan, D'scover, under the label YGEX and embarked on his first Japanese tour. The following year, he released two more albums, D'slove and his first chart-topper, Delight. D'slove Tour made him the first Korean soloist to gather over 100,000 fans for two Japanese tours in a row. In 2017, he held his first solo Japanese dome tour, performing for over 150,000 people from four shows, and released his second number one album, D-Day, that made him the second foreign male solo artist to have two consecutive number one albums in Japan after Michael Jackson.

In 2018, Daesung enlisted into the miliatry, along side fellow members G-dragon and Taeyang. He was released in late 2019, and has yet to make any releases. However, Daesung has a personal YouTube channel that he regularly updates, called "D'splay". He has yet to hint at the possibility of new music in the near future.

T.O.P

Stage name: T.O.P (톱)
Real name: Choi Seung-Hyun (최승현)
Born: 4 November 1987
Height: 181 cm (5'11")
Weight: 65 kg (143lbs)
Blood type: B

T.O.P is the lead rapper and oldest member in Big Bang. He is a talented song writer is heavily involved in the song-writing in Big Bang's songs. He is the 'visual' of the group, known for his good looks and unique rapping style. He has a husky and powerful voice. His presence in the industry has made having a deep-voiced member in K-pop groups a trend. His stage name, "T.O.P" was given to him by his senior, SE7EN, meaning the peak. It was inspired by one of T.O.P's favourite rappers, B.I.G. He is well-respected in the K-pop industry.

Seung-Hyun is also a talented actor. He has starred in over 10 movies, along side his music career. He made his acting debut in 2007, with the movie "I am Sam". Since then, he has starred in many others such as the popular movie, "Tazza: The Hidden Card".

The rapper has yet to release a solo album. However, he made his official solo debut in 2010 with his first single "Turn it Up", which was part of the album "GD & TOP", in which he collaborated with fellow band member, G-dragon. The single had over 1.3 million sales in Korea, and peaked at number one on Gaon Charts. He released his second single in 2013, "Doom Dada", which would sell a over 600,000 copies in Korea. Doom Dada would go on to top the Gaon Charts, and was high-praised by critics for its unqiue and catchy sound.

In 2017, he was enlisted into the military, but was only released in 2019 due to complications. He was inactive up until 2022, when he announced he would be releasing his long awaited first solo album, along with his own wine brand, "T-Spot", which is set to be released in December 2022.
